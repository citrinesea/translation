Translation Notes

  1. ↑ お兄ちゃん (onii-chan) is an honorific for one's older brother. It can also be used for young men that younger children look up to, hence "Mister".
  2. ↑ Minatomirai (みなとみらい) is a city center located in central Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture. It goes by "The Harbor of the Future".
  3. ↑ Castella, a Japanese spongecake. While based on cakes brought by Portuguese merchants in the 1700s, it is considered a traditional Japanese sweet because of its evolution to fit Japanese tastes.
