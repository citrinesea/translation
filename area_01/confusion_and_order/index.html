Confusion and Order 2

Some years back. In a slum in a certain state in America.

Very impressive, Nanashi.1 You’ve saved me a lot of trouble.

Catching a vagrant on the run is easy enough, but I’d rather not waste the energy I have.

— All “I” merely did was my job.

Indeed. That you did.

However, there are far too many things in this world that cannot be “merely done”.

I’m impressed with your work ethic. I’ll be counting on you next time, Nanashi.

... I told you to stop calling me by that name.

I call you that because I have nothing else to call you. I just need something to call you, whether it’s a fitting name or not.

And so I’ve chosen to call you “Nanashi”. I’ve adopted it from the placeholder name reigning from your home country of Japan of “Nameless Gonbei”—“Nanashi no Gonbei.”2

Doesn’t that make for a nice pair? Nameless Gonbei and John Doe.

...

Oh, could it be you would have preferred “Gonbei”? I had the impression it differed from the image you had in mind...

Enough. Let’s keep moving.

It’s that district again. I imagine this area’ll prove to be a difficult one, so how about we bring this one back and take care of him together?

That’ll just be a waste of our time.

Hmm... Well, I’ll call over my lackeys then. Give me a moment—

(— What am I doing in a place like this?)

(Maintaining public order within a slum sounds good on paper, but...)

(In reality, it’s nothing but squeezing pennies out of scumbags in the language this place knows best.)

(And... if our management requires greater force, we procure further manpower accordingly.)

(A city of splendor—and behind it, a heap of outcasts thrown out from it. And my job—to manage the bastards that squat in this dump.)

(I cannot comprehend how these people willingly choose to live in a place like this. These guys continue on living, even if it means crawling along the ground and gulping down muddy water.)

(... No, as if I’m in any place to speak.)

(I myself broke out into the outside world, unable to accept the world I had previously lived in. ... Perhaps it would be more accurate to say that I ran away.)

(Anywhere would have sufficed—anywhere but that place. No reality out there would be more unbearable to face than that one.)

(And so I decided to survive by any means necessary, and... I left into a world where I wanted to live.)

(And here is the result. I stand beside those who oppress the weak, living out my days, one at a time.)

(After all, that’s just how reality is.)

Haah... Even still, it’s far better than being at that place.

Fade to black...

The amount here falls short of what we asked of you. And here I’d already granted you an extension and everything...

Hand over the amount, in full. If you don’t have enough, then rob somebody, or whatever it is you do. You got this money through shady means too, didn’t you? Or... do you intend to make an enemy out of him?
