Chapter 6

Season: Autumn

Anzu-dono! I have come as you requested!

Hasumi-dono and I were needed for our support, but what sort of business do you have with us in particular?

Do you need guidance on using a sword for iaido? Anzu-dono has been captivated with swords too, hm, happy day, happy day ♪

If that were the case, I don't imagine she'd need to call for me.

She's shaking her head "no". Kanzaki is hard enough to manage, it'd be intolerable for Anzu to act strangely too.

Eichi had gotten in touch with me to tell me he was participating in "Toyland," or such. The offical paperwork was approved without fuss.

If it were student council business, then there would be no need to call for Kanzaki... Unless it's to do with AKATSUKI? But then, I can't see a reason why Kiryu wouldn't be called.

Hm~mm. I have no idea. I don't understand your intentions.

Please forgive me for my tardiness. … Oh? Vice President-sama? Kanzaki-sama?

Fushimi…? Did Anzu call you as well?

No, Anzu was just guiding me on how to make a teddy bear…

Hmm hmm. You invited Vice President-sama and Kanzaki-sama here in order to guide me in creating designs?

Teddy bear? What are you talking about? Explain it from the very beginning.

This is my selfishness. That is to say… While I wasn’t around, Bocchama was a good boy, so this is his reward.

I’ve set my mind on it— I would like to make a teddy bear just for Bocchama.

However, I have no artistic skill. Anzu-san happened to be making merchandise, so she offered to assist me.

I understand the situation. I... do know a little about drawing, so I'm sure I can help you, Fushimi.

I am not well-acquainted with art myself, but I admire the fact you would try to overcome your weaknesses for the sake of your master. Poor as it may be, I will help you to the best of my ability.

Vice President-sama, Kanzaki-sama, thank you from the bottom of my heart. This humble Yuzuru is most fortunate.

After all, the level of Fushimi's art... is beyond original. Indescribably so.

I recall being shown a mock portrait of Himemiya once.

You remembered it well, hm. I put everything I had into making it.

But Vice President-sama said that it was, "sentient vegetables cannibalizing each other," and even Tsukasa-sama said it was a drawing that was, "unnerving to look at."

Since then, Anzu-san has been my gracious instructor, so I hope I've started to improve a little.

At any rate, I am going to sketch a picture. Now to start on the shape of Momo-sama's companion... Heheh, how adorable ♪

Is that a bear…? Maybe a squirrel…? No, it’s neither man nor beast. It has a strange scraggliness to it and thin, slender eyes. Could this be some new kind of youkai?

Well, I’ll add some ribbons to the ears.

Momo-sama wears western clothes as well, so if I add these too, it will make for a lovely teddy bear ♪

All I can see is a youkai that looks as if it will to pounce at any moment.

Hm, it should be a teddy bear as adorable as Bocchama himself, just as I envisioned.

It sounds as if it's a bloodthirsty beast, hm? Well, I suppose Bocchama won't be pleased with that.

Yes. Never have I seen such a mystical picture such as this. It has a frightfulness that is unlike a picture scroll of vengeful ghosts.

This picture unsettles my heart. It would make good work if used as a test of courage, but if you do not intend to frighten others, you'd ought to seal it away.

Though, in that case, it would just tread on Fushimi's spirit.

I'll draw a model sketch for you to reference off of. It shouldn’t be that hard to copy, even for you.

Please forgive me for the inconvenience, Vice President-sama.

Hmph. You aren’t a member of the student council, yet you lend your help with work that has to be done. If this is all there is to do, it’s nothing.

I suppose Toyland is the temporary unit fine and 2wink are forming.

I have work to do for the student council and AKATSUKI, so I can’t participate in the Dream Fes, but I suppose I can help if it’s just about selling merchandise.

I will help too, of course. From manual labor to odd jobs, leave anything to me ♪
