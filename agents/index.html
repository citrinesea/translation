Chapter 1

Season: Winter
Location: Student Council Room

Hello, Anzu-chan.

Welcome to the Student Council room, come make yourself at home. Care for a cup of tea? It's even colder this time of year, right, so it's to keep your body warm...♪

What's the matter? Please, there's nothing to be afraid of, you know. That hurts my feelings, fufufu...

Well, I suppose I am pushing unreasonable demands of you. Of course you can't help but be wary.

Yes, anyways, please take a seat. I don't intend to take up too much of your time, so you can make yourself comfortable.

(Cough, cough). I just get in terrible shape during the winter, don't I...?

This time last year I was in the hospital. I've holed up in the Student Council room where it's heated. just to be safe.

The other student council members are bustling all over the place with work, as well. Iit's lonely having no one to talk to.

I'm truly grateful you came when I called for you.

Anyways. I didn't call you for the sake of idle pratter, but I don't mind if we have tea. First, I have a favor to ask of you, would you hear me out?

Ahh, please don't be on guard. This isn't me asking as the Student Council president, it's me asking as a normal person.

Isn't listening to those sorts of things your duty, Producer-chan?

In return for giving you such a delicious blend, allow me to give you a little task to do for me ♪

Anzu-chan. I am going to give you a secret mission.

Please keep all things regarding this secret mission confidental from everyone else. The other student council members... and especially Keito cannot know.

Do you understand everything I've said so far?

It seems they are making some suspicious moves of their own...

We can eye each other down and try to ascertain each other's intentions, but I want my goal to be accomplished. This is something I can only ask of you.

Shall it I put it in another way? Anzu-chan, I want you to be my secret agent.

I may be the student council president, but I was hospitalized for quite a long time, wasn't I...?

The one who truly holds the power around here is Keito. Most of the members of the student council are Keito's confidantes.

Keito handles all of the practical matters of the student council after all. It's the fruit of all his hard work and dedication.

I have been absent that entire time, so I can't just take that away from him now.

Be that as it may, it has become a hindrance this time. The Student Council holds complete dominion over the halls of Yumenosaki Academy

The walls have ears and the doors have eyes, if you will.

I would like for you to carry out the secret mission by slipping past those eyes and ears.

You've colluded with Trickstar before, so you chipped away cracks into the steel authority of the Student Council haven't you?

That is what I mean when I say you are the only one who can do this. I expect you can do that, so I requested it of you specifically.

I don't say that as some sort of dig, you know?

I judge you very highly. That's why I'm bowing my head and asking for your help like this. I truly am in trouble, so please, if you would, lend me a hand.

Of course, the end of the year is packed with DreamFes like StarFes and SS. As a Producer, you have plates of your own to juggle, I'm sure.

So, just keep my "secret mission" in the back of your mind...

If you have the time to spare, I'd appreciate if you could handle it. I'll be operating on my own, so you're just a precaution.

I have high hopes for you, Anzu-chan. Why don't we do the best we can to have a wonderful Christmas Eve together. ... Now, would you care for a second helping of tea?

Chapter 2

Location: Archery Hall

... And that is the extent of my order. Do you have any questions, Isara?

Mn~ ... No, there's no doubts for me really, but... I get how you feel, having to worry about a childhood friend, y'know~?

Hmph. Your childhood friend is Sakuma's little brother, right? Take this as advice from someone who has experience: getting involved with those vampires is bad news.

It seems you were born under the same unlucky stars as me, huh, Isara?

Well, it's boring if things don't happen. Lately, I've been thinking it's fun in its own way.

... I guess that's that. Should I go start getting the ball rolling?

Yes, I trust you with this. Just do things "your way," there's no rush to get it done. I have full faith in your capabilities, Isara.

Even when you say that, I'm still a little worried~ The person I'm talking to is the Student Council President after all.

It won't be easy to do the normal way, surely. Can't you move yourself, Vice President?

I'll be making my own moves, but I don't have enough hands. I need to focus entirely on preparations for the year's end StarFes and SS.

And there are countless DreamFeses of all kinds on top of that.

The B1s Eichi seems to amuse himself with... Those informal quarrels are encouraged too. There have been rumors of S3's and such being approaved as official matches too.

The frequency of DreamFeses have absolutely exploded. It's impossible for the Student Council to supervise and manage each and every one of them.

From next year on, the Production Course will properly start up... So I imagine the load of the student council will decrease then.

I can't use the valuable personnel of the Student Council for a personal matter like this.

Huh~ I'm being used for a personal matter, huh? Exploited as always, huh.

Don't say something like that, Isara. I'm relying on you to being able to accomplish this if you've got the free time from your usual work.

... I think you'll be the next student council president, you know.

Mn~ I'm happy you think so highly of me, but... if you give me too much responsibility, I won't be able to have as much freedom, which would suck.

Well, no, it's a nice change of pace. The Vice President, relying on me. If it's an order from you, I'll the job done... This time too ♪

Thanks, you're really helping me out. For now, i have Archery Club duties to attend to.

You're the only one I can rely on, Isara. I'll be sure to thank you. I'm sorry to have to give you work at such an important time right before "SS" begins.

No, no. stuff like this helps keep my mind off things, somwhow. People have been real considerate of me lately, and haven't been asking for things. It actually gets kinda lonely.

I wanna be a little more busy...♪

That sounds like you've already got a disease. Well, whatever, I'm relying on you.

So, just to be clear, Eichi absolutely cannot know about this.

That guy's been real... Seems like he's been calling that Anzu over often. Watch out for her too.

Anzu has? She's always got sucha blank look on her face, but I think she's the type who can't really keep a secret. Well, I understand, I'll keep an eye out.

Is there anything else for me to do? I can get that job done next~♪

You're a real energetic, aren't you...Well, Himemiya seemed troubled about something, so if you have the time, could you check in with him.

Well, people are very susceptible to getting ill this time of year, so don't push yourself too much.

Yeah, yeah. You're prone to pushing yourself too, so I was honestly glad you gave me the job, you know. ... Anyways, I'm off right awa~y♪

Right. If anything happens, report back, I always have my cellphone on. Well, if it's you handling it, I can rest easy...♪
