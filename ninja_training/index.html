Chapter 2

Location: Classroom 2A

Transfer Student-dono, are you here~?

Ohh, Transfer Student-dono. It’s good that I found you before you left the classroom~

To tell you the truth, I wanted you to accompany me for ninja training, so I came to ask you if that was alright.

As expected of Transfer Student-dono! I just knew you would accompany my pursuit of knowledge…!!

Well then, please follow after me~♪

Location: Rooftop

…! Transfer Student-dono, we’re so lucky~♪ No one’s here~

This time of day, a lot of units practice on the roof, and it’s difficult to ask people to hand over their spot for the purposes of “ninja-training”.

So I always train hard on the edge of the sports grounds instead.

But because the Archangel known as Transfer Student-dono is here with me today, fortune has come my way, too~

Ah, don’t retreat as if you’re saying, “Don’t call me Archangel”! Come baaack~!

A-Anyway, what I mean to say is, with a powerful ally such as you, Transfer Student-dono, I won’t face defeat!

I’ll become a splendid ninja and make sure the association gets more members~!!

Hm? Transfer Student-dono. You seem to have a curious look on your face, did you want to ask me something?

Ah, you want to know the reason why I don’t practice in a classroom, but outdoors instead… Erm, that would be because there aren’t enough club members, so I’m not able to reserve a room.

If I could get the Association promoted to club-status, then I could use a clubroom to my heart’s content for ninja training, but…

First off, I’d have to gather enough members, but even if I were to manage that, I was told by the teachers that I need at least a year of club activities2 before I’m able to get it promoted…

The Ninja Association that I established hasn’t been active for a full year, and on top of that, there aren’t enough members.

So my goal is to gain more members before I graduate, raise its status from an association to a club, and then accomplish my plan to transform the student body into ninjas!

As the first step, I’d like for Transfer Student-dono to be enlisted as a member of the Ninja Association…

Huh, Transfer Student-dono? Transfer Student-dono–?

That's strange. She was just right in front of me... Ah, there you were, Transfer Student-dono.

…!? Don’t run away! Your face says “I can’t take this any longer”... Don’t leave me~!

I promise I won't try to recruit you again, Transfer Student-dono! So, please, come with me to my training! That's all I ask! I'm begging you...!

Ohh, you'll come!? I'm overjoyed with gratitude~!

Well then, Transfer Student-dono, here I go~♪

Shutatatata… Shutatatata…

Leg strength and jumping power are absolutely essential for a ninja. Training your leg strength like this is the first step on the path to becoming a ninja~

Not just that, but completely erasing the sound of your footsteps is crucial for ninjas.

It doesn’t matter how fast you run — If the enemy picks up on your footsteps, you’re done for.

What do you think, Transfer Student-dono? Am I managing to properly dash across the field like the breeze?

If there’s anything wrong, point it out, even if it’s just the teeniest bit off-point~

Eh? You can’t tell whether my footsteps are making a sound because I'm talking while running?

What! I made such a rudimentary mistake… What a critical blunder of a lifetime!

Understood. I’ll try running without talking~

Shutatatata… Shutatatata…

Transfer Student-dono, how about that!? Ahh, “shutatatata" is my catchphrase when I run!

Try to run without saying “shutatatata”? Th-That’s going to be difficult, but for the sake of becoming a ninja, I’ll do my best~!

Because to me, Transfer Student-dono is a producer and Archangel-dono~♪
