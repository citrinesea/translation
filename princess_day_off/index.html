Translation Notes

  1. ↑ Amigurumi (あみぐるみ) are small crocheted stuffed animals.
  2. ↑ Shishou (-師匠) is an honorific used for a master (usually in a specific craft). This is the first time we see Esu call Madara this.
  3. ↑ Daishishou (-大師匠). If -shishou means "master", -daishishou means "grandmaster".
