Prologue

It's been decided, there's new work for Hibiki and the theatre! This'll be busy, busy indeed...

Ah— Producer! Good mo~rning ♪

Yeah, good morning. You two are here early! Is Hibiki already here too, by any chance?

Whether it's the best beaches or the best food, I'll give everyone the finest hospitality—! Okinawa is a place that energizes you after all!

Ah, looks like she's already here! I see, hospitality... let's go in, everyone!

This is Arisa's Green Room Dash! Hibiki-chan, what was this you were talking about giving hospitali~ty!?

Ah, Arisa! Erena and Producer, you guys too, look at this picture!

Mnmn, A hibiscus flower with a blue sky and sea... C— Could this be Okinawa perchance? The glorious Holy Land that brought forth Hibiki-chan!?

Glorious Holy Land, huh...! Yep, that's the magnificent beach I used to play at tons! Pretty, isn't it?

Well, I don't know if "glorious" is the word to use here, but... it really is such a beautiful beach.

Isn't it nice~ I want to go to Okinawa too~oo.

Really? That's why I was just saying that if we went to Okinawa, I'd treat everyone as much as I could ♪

What a perfect segue, huh. You wouldn't just be going to Okinawa, but you'd probably be able to treat everyone too, Hibiki!

Huh? But if we're not going to Okinawa, how am I supposed to treat everyone? Is this a riddle?

It's not a riddle... The truth is, a request has come in for you to be a Haisai[^] Ambassador, a poster girl for the Okinawa Fair![^]

"Haisai Ambassador"? What's that!?

Apparently, a major shopping mall is collaborating with Okinawa to put together the large-scale Okinawa Fair.

They'd like you to do promotional work for Okinawa as a poster girl!

Eh...? Me? A poster girl!?

Ohh...! So she's going to become a poster girl for Okinawa!? Isn't that amazing, Hibiki!?

What's more, due to the other party's wishes, it looks like the song for the commercial is going to be Hibiki's "Pon De Beach".

My song's gonna be used in commercial!?

That's right. During the campaign, you'll go to a big shopping mall in the area. After the live, there will be a meet-and-greet with fans.

Of course, you'll have to make time during all that to perform at the theatre! It'll be busy busy indeed!

Yay~!! Thank you, Producer!

I gathered all of you here today because I wanted you to help Hibiki with the campaign. Can I count on you?

Yes! is there even a question?! Hibiki-chan, the campaign girl... I have a real good feeling that I'll get some really great photos out of this.

With something like a commercial shoot, that's an awfully big campaign they're running, huh? That's amazing, Hibiki-chan...!

Hibiki's "Pon de Beach"...? Wasn't that a pretty tough song?

There's nothing to worry about, Noriko~! If it's the dance, Hibiki and I will show you how to do it~♪

Thank you so much, everyone! I look forward to working with you!

Al~righty, I've made up my mind! I'll do my very best to work towards my goal, too~!

You have some kind of goal, Hibiki?

Of course I do! I want everyone to come and experience the beaches of Okinawa!

It'd be nice if "Pon de Beach" could make everyone feel like they're having fun... Ehehe.

I see. That's a great goal, isn't it?

Producer... Alright! I'm gonna do my best~!
