Chapter 1

Season: Winter
Location: Classroom

Wahh~ Looking right, looking left…~ No one’s here..

Er, did I come here a little too early? But I guess if people were here in the classroom, I might not be able to concentrate on my script.

If anything, the peace and quiet’s even better for focus. Alright, let's hurry up and get this done before everyone gets here~♪

Morning~♪

(Like I thought, no one’s here. Yeah, I'm the first one~♪)

………

(“Spread out the writing paper,” I said. “Let’s write!” I said. But I’ve got my pen and… I’m at a total loss. I have no clue what to write.(

(Up ‘til now… Or rather, since Anzu-chan enrolled here at school, the Broadcasting Committee didn’t have that much work to do.)

(Of course, we did stuff like picking what plays for the lunchtime broadcast and making news announcements, but...)

(There were regulations the student council gave us, so we couldn't do whatever we wanted. It always felt like the same pattern.)

(I didn't wanna do that, so I’d go to the shopping district by school to find some cheap, good tracks.)

(But in the DDD, fine and its leader, the student council president Tenshouin-senpai, fell, and the absolute monarchy went with it.)

(DreFes has changed a lot with the (restoration/revival) of the temporary unit system and the establishment of S3s)

(Now, we can broadcast whatever we want without worrying about what the student council says, so Nito-senpai’s really excited about it.)

(The other day, he was writing a script for an in-house television program. He was doing his best gathering all materials and deciding on the program, even though he was sleep-deprived.)

(Compared to that, I’m not any good…? I keep relying on Nito-senpai, I can’t even write the lunch broadcast script from scratch.)

… No, I can’t just get depressed about this! I’m on duty the day after tomorrow, so I have to lock in and get it done!

(To make sure Nito-senpai can rest easy and leave things to us, me and Shinobu-kun have to prove we can reliably do it ourselves～♪)

Aaalrighty, (I can do it/I’m feeling fired up)! The theme… I’d rather it be more fun to listen to than stuffy.

Fun content, fun content… what makes me feel like I’m having fun? When I’m eating?

A tier list of favorite foods might be good!

Heheh, I’m right on the money! Sure enough, work that needs patience is important!
