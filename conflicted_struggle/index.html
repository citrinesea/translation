Translation Notes

  1. ↑ An Okinawan sweet deep-fried bun, similar to a donut.
  2. ↑ Uchinaaguchi is one of the native languages of Okinawa. The language utilizes elongated sounds quite frequently, so Ibuki might be suggesting the cadence is unfamiliar to a mainlander.
  3. ↑ Oshikatsu (推し活; lit. lifestyle supporting a favorite) encompasses all the different ways someone interacts with or supports their "favorite" (oshi).
