Tetora Substory 2

Original Release on April 28, 2015
Characters
Writer
Yuuki Yoshino
Translation
citrinesea

This is bad! Super-duper bad~!

Ooh, there you are Transfer Student-san! He~ya!

If you’re here in the library this early in the morning, you must be real serious! You’re a real model student~!

Huh? I need to be more quiet in the library? Oh, you’re totally right! I’ll zip my mouth. Shhh…

Wait, like this I’m not gonna be able to speak at all!?

It’s okay if we whisper? You’re a real smart cookie, huh, Transfer Student-san!

Oh yeah, why are you in the library anyways? You’re studying, right? You’re a can-do senior that stands above the rest, huh!

Me? Oh, I’m… Well, it's a really depressing story.

I totally flunked a quiz the other day...~

And I heard that if I flunk the next one, I’m not gonna be allowed to partake in club activities

That fateful quiz is almost here…! And even worse, I suck at classical Japanese

— Not even, I suck at modern Japanese, classical Japanese, math, social studies, geography, English— I suck at everything and anything they could offer in this school!

Anyways, that’s the deal, and I’m in real dire straits~...

…! Didn’t you say you came to study, Transfer Student-san!?

So, yeah! That means you have to be good at studying, right!? Transfer Student-san! Please, teach me classical Japanese, please—!

Huh? You’re not very good at it either?

And more importantly, you have a test you flunked, so you came to the library to study for that…?

Oh no! I’m screwed…! I might as well kiss club activities goodbye, my test is today…

S—Seriously!? You would help me study instead of working on your own studies?

T–Transfer Student-san… No, please lemme call you Anego!1

To me, you’re no ordinary person!

Coming to someone’s aid even though you have your own problems on your plate— no ordinary person could do that!

You’re just like Taishou, you got a big ol’ heart. I’m so glad I ran into you Transfer Student-san!

Ah, I’m sorry! I started talkin’ loud again, I’m completely useless, huh.

… Anego. Here and here and here. And then… all these I didn’t get either, so please show me how to do them!

That’s too many? Mn~nn… Well, then just this one, this one, that one, and that one.

Anego, please take good care of me! I’m gonna avoid flunking out, whatever it takes~♪

  1. ↑ Anego is like "aniki", where it means "big sis" in the sense of a boss-like older sister figure.
